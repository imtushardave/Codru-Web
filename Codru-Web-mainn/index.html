Services

1 on 1 care

Each child will be supervised with one on one live interaction among students. Child's own pace and growth will be taken care of on individual basis.

No Rote Learning

Instead of word for word learning, a deeper approach to the topic will be the method of teaching for better and long term understanding of the subject.

Planned Curriculum

With carefully planned curriculum while keeping the needs of a child in mind we take an interesting unique take on each topic to make learning more enjoyable for your child.

Performance Evaluation

Regular tests will be taken for assessment of the child according to which our course will be modified on frequent basis to meet the needs of child.
