Services

Live Teaching with Recorded classes

The key aspect of classes is live interaction with the child on daily basis where they will be provided with class recordings for life time access.

Customized Problem Solving

Each child will be given homework based on their individuality, keeping their growth & development in mind. The notes provided will be to the point for fast revision & better learning of concepts.

1 on 1 Mentorship

Each child will be supervised one on one at regular intervals to determine their pace & growth & to give them guidance to perform better & efficiently.

No Rote Learning

A deeper approach to the topic with the method of teaching of word to word learning for better & long term understanding of the subject.

Planned Curriculum

With carefully planned curriculum while keeping the needs of a child in mind we take an interesting unique take on each topic to make learning more enjoyable for your child.

Performance Evaluation

Regular tests will be taken for assessment of the child according to which our course will be modified on frequent basis to meet the needs of child.

Accepting Enviroment

The child will experience a judgement free enviroment where they can ask their doubts without fear or second guessing themselves. Each & every child's learning will be an individual concern.
